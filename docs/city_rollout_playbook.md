# City Rollout Playbook: Jamshedpur, Ranchi, Dhanbad

## Objective
Launch and stabilize supply + demand in three priority cities with measurable weekly operating targets and a repeatable city management rhythm.

---

## 1) City-wise Target Counts (First 12 Weeks)

> **Definition guardrails**
> - **Verified teachers** = KYC complete + profile approved + demo class quality check passed.
> - **Active students** = at least one attended session in last 30 days.
> - **Weekly bookings** = total successful class bookings in the week.

### Target Table

| City | Week 4: Verified Teachers | Week 8: Verified Teachers | Week 12: Verified Teachers | Week 4: Active Students | Week 8: Active Students | Week 12: Active Students | Week 4: Weekly Bookings | Week 8: Weekly Bookings | Week 12: Weekly Bookings |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| **Jamshedpur** | 35 | 70 | 110 | 120 | 260 | 420 | 160 | 360 | 620 |
| **Ranchi** | 45 | 90 | 140 | 160 | 340 | 560 | 220 | 470 | 820 |
| **Dhanbad** | 30 | 60 | 95 | 100 | 220 | 360 | 130 | 300 | 520 |

### Why these targets
- **Ranchi** gets the highest target due to larger student ecosystem and stronger college density.
- **Jamshedpur** is balanced: strong industrial middle-class demand with manageable onboarding.
- **Dhanbad** starts leaner but should scale with focused offline + referral loops.

## 3) Offline Activation Cadence

## Weekly Cadence per City

| Activation Type | Events / Week / City | Typical Audience | Expected Lead-to-Profile Conversion | Expected Profile-to-Verified Conversion | Output Expectation (per city/week) |
|---|---:|---|---:|---:|---|
| Teacher onboarding meetups | 2 | 25–40 teachers/event | 70% | 55% | 20–30 verified teachers/month |
| Student demo camps | 2 | 40–60 students/event | 60% | 35% (to first booking) | 65–95 first bookings/month |
| Parent counselling kiosks | 1 | 30–50 parents/event | 45% | 30% (to trial class) | Better trust + retention uplift |
| Hyperlocal referral drives | 1 | Existing users + neighborhood | 50% | 40% | 20–40 incremental weekly bookings |

## City-specific cadence emphasis
- **Jamshedpur:** prioritize student demo camps near Sakchi/Bistupur and teacher meetups in Adityapur/Telco.
- **Ranchi:** run one extra college-linked student activation every alternate week.
- **Dhanbad:** heavier parent counselling in first 6 weeks to reduce trial drop-off.

## 4) Demand Partnerships (Colleges / Coaching / Telegram Admins)

## Jamshedpur – First Demand Targets
- Colleges: Karim City College, Jamshedpur Women’s College, Co-operative College.
- Coaching clusters: Sakchi test-prep lane, Bistupur PCM/commerce centers, Mango school-support centers.
- Telegram admins: city exam prep channels, neighborhood tuition groups, parent community channels.

## Ranchi – First Demand Targets
- Colleges: Ranchi University affiliated colleges, St. Xavier’s College (Ranchi), Ranchi Women’s College.
- Coaching clusters: Lalpur competitive-prep institutions, Kanke Road NEET/JEE coaching belt, Harmu school tuition centers.
- Telegram admins: Jharkhand exam alert channels, Ranchi coaching update groups, district parent help forums.

## Dhanbad – First Demand Targets
- Colleges: PK Roy Memorial College, SSLNT Women’s College, Binod Bihari Mahto Koyalanchal University ecosystem.
- Coaching clusters: Bank More entrance prep centers, Hirapur commerce/math tutors, Jharia school-support coaching lanes.
- Telegram admins: Dhanbad study circles, board exam preparation groups, area-wise parent advisory communities.

### Demand Partnership Offer Structure
- Co-branded free workshop every 2 weeks.
- Performance-based referral payout after first paid booking.
- Priority teacher matching SLA (within 24 hours) for partner leads.
- Shared performance dashboard snapshot every week.

- Protect student experience: speed-to-match and first-class quality are core retention levers.
